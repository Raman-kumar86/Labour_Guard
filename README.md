# Labour Guard -- Labour Law Compliance Platform

## Overview

**Labour Guard** is a web-based platform designed to promote labour law
awareness and compliance while minimizing unnecessary intrusion into
workers' privacy. The platform educates workers and organizations about
labour rights, ethical workplace practices, and legal compliance.

This project demonstrates how technology can support worker welfare,
promote transparency, and encourage responsible labour management.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Objectives

-   Spread awareness about labour rights and workplace regulations
-   Provide accessible information regarding labour law compliance
-   Promote ethical workplace practices
-   Reduce unnecessary monitoring or intrusion into worker privacy
-   Balance compliance with worker dignity

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Features

-   Labour Law Awareness -- Information about important labour rights
    and regulations
-   Worker-Focused Design -- Simple and easy-to-understand resources
-   Privacy Respecting Approach -- Compliance without excessive
    surveillance
-   User-Friendly Interface -- Accessible and clean web interface
-   Educational Resources -- Guides related to labour protection laws
